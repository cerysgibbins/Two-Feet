Tap Dance

Tap Dance is formed by using one persons foot hitting the floor to construct a form of music. The shoe of metal makes the creative rhythmic pattern itself while generating the feet. There are different variations of tap, these are rhythm tap and Broadway tap. The rhythm tap focuses more on the musicality and the broadway tap focuses on the dance.
