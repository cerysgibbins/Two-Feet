Ballet Dance

(3) Ballet was originally discovered in the Italian Renaissance courts in the 15th century. Ballet can be one of the most challenging dances to learn as it takes many years to require training to learn and master the movements. A well known example of a ballet production that includes choreography and music is Swan Lake.

Even though ballet is its own genre in dance there are also different styles of ballet. These are Classical, Neoclassical and Contemporary.
